5 Policy Coverage/benefits payable • Benefits payable on Death:

➢In the case of the death of the Life Insured anytime during Policy Term, provided the Policy is in force and is not a Lapsed Policy or in Reduced Paid Up Mode, we will pay Sum Assured on Death and Terminal Bonus, if any (“Death Benefit”) to the Claimant. However, in case You have opted for Policy Continuance Benefit, no Terminal Bonus shall be payable at the time of death.

➢Any Survival Benefit paid after the death of the Life Insured, but before the date of intimation thereof, will be reduced from the Death Benefit payable. However, in case You have opted for Policy Continuance Benefit, Survival Benefit paid will not be reduced.

Clause 1.1 of Part C
• Survival Benefit excluding that payable on maturity:

➢During the Policy Term while the Life Insured is alive and all due Premiums have been received, Survival Benefit payable shall depend on the Survival Benefit option chosen by You and shall be payable on the date as specified in the Schedule, in arrears or advance (as chosen by You).

➢Early Income Option -In case You have chosen the Early Income Option, then we will pay the following Survival Benefits:
  1. Cash Bonus, if any, payable starting from the 2nd (Second) Policy Year till end of Policy Term; and,
  2. Guaranteed Income, payable starting from 2nd (Second) Policy Year, during the Guarantee Period.
Note:If the Survival Benefit frequency is monthly and in arrears, then the payout will start from the end of 13th Policy month and in case the Survival Benefit frequency is Annual and in arrears, then the payout will start from the end of 24th month from the Date of Commencement of Risk.
➢Early Income with Guaranteed Money Back Option -If You have chosen this option, we will pay the following Survival Benefits:
  1. Cash Bonus, if any, payable starting from the 2nd (Second) Policy Year till end of Policy Term; and,
  2. Guaranteed Money Back, payable at the end of 1st (First), 6th (Sixth) and 11th (Eleventh) Policy Years, all after the end of Premium Payment Term.
Note:If the frequency of Survival Benefit is monthly and in arrears, Cash Benefit payout will start from the end of 13th Policy month and in case the Survival Benefit frequency is Annual and in arrears, then the payout will start from the end of 24th month from the Date of Commencement of Risk and Guaranteed Money Back payout will be made annually.
➢Deferred Income Option –If You have chosen this option, we will pay the following Survival Benefits:
  1. Cash bonus, if any, payable starting from 2nd (Second) Policy Year after Premium Payment Term till end of Policy Term; and
  2. Guaranteed Income, shall be payable starting from 2nd (Second) Policy Year after Premium Payment Term, during the Guarantee Period.
Note:If the Survival Benefit frequency is monthly and in arrears, then the payout will start from the end of 13th month after the end of Premium Payment Term and in case the Survival Benefit frequency is Annual and in arrears, then the payout will start from the end of 24th month from the end of Premium Payment Term.
 Clause 1.2 of Part C
• Policy Continuance Benefit:

➢If You have opted for “Policy Continuance Benefit”, then on death of the Life Insured any time during the Policy Term, Sum Assured on Death shall be paid immediately as a lump sum amount and the applicable future Survival Benefits (including Cash Bonus) and Maturity Benefit shall be paid as and when due, without payment of any future Premiums. No Terminal Bonus shall be payable at the time of death.

➢Policy Continuance Benefit shall not be available in case the Policy is in Reduced Paid Up Mode. Thus, in case of death of the Life Insured while the Policy is in Reduced Paid Up Mode, Reduced Paid Up Sum Assured on Death shall be paid and the Policy shall terminate.

➢If You have opted for Policy Continuance Benefit, then upon death of the Policyholder, the Claimant shall have a right to discontinue the Policy anytime in which case the Claimant shall be entitled to receive an amount equal to the applicable Surrender Value, subject to the provisions of Clause 1 of Part D of the policy document, as would have been payable to You, had You been alive and surrendered the Policy.

 Clause 1.1.3 of Part C
•Maturity Benefits

➢On survival of the Life Insured on the Maturity Date, if the insurance cover under this Policy is in force and the Policy is not under Reduced Paid Up Mode, We will pay the sum total of the following in lump sum to the Claimant:

  1. Sum Assured on Maturity;
  2. Terminal Bonus, if any; and
  3. Any accrued Survival Benefits, if not already paid;

9 Exclusions (events where insurance coverage is not payable), if any. Brief list of the applicable exclusions, if any:
➢ Suicide Exclusion:
If the Life Insured commits suicide, within 12 (Twelve) months from the Date of Inception of Policy or from the date of Revival of this Policy, all risks and benefits under this Policy shall cease and the Policy shall terminate immediately and We shall only pay the following to the Claimant, provided the policy is in force:
  1. higher of the Surrender Value as on the date of death or the sum of Total Premiums Paid, loadings for modal premium and Underwriting Extra Premium paid till the date of death received by Us, if the Policy has acquired a Surrender Value; or
  2. sum of Total Premiums Paid, loadings for modal premium and Underwriting Extra Premium if any, till the date of death received by Us, if the Policy has not acquired a Surrender Value.

13 Lapse, paid-up and revival of the Policy • Grace Period:
A grace period of thirty (30) days (fifteen (15) days in case of monthly premium payment mode) from the due date for payment of each premium will be allowed to the Policyholder for payment of contractual premium. During the grace period, the Company will accept the premium without interest. The insurance coverage continues during the grace period but if the Life Insured dies during the grace period, the Company will deduct the due premium (if any) till the date of death from the benefits payable under the Policy.
 Clause 7 of Part C
• Lapse Period
➢ In case of non- receipt of first year's full premium, the Policy will lapse, and no benefits shall be payable.
Clause 7 of Part D
• Paid Up
➢ Reduced Paid Up Benefits:
If this Policy has acquired a Surrender Value, then, in the event of non-payment of the due Premiums by You to Us, on the expiry of Grace Period, this Policy will not lapse and will continue under Reduced Paid Up Mode unless Revived and the following benefits will be payable:
a.Reduced Paid up Death Benefit:In the event of the death of the Life Insured during Policy Term, We will pay Reduced Paid Up Sum Assured on Death. In case the option of accrual of Survival Benefits has been chosen by You, accrued Survival Benefit, if any and if not already paid, shall be paid additionally. However, the minimum Death Benefit shall be at least 105% (One Hundred Five percent) of sum of Total Premiums Paid plus Underwriting Extra Premium plus loadings for modal premiums received till the date of death of the Life Insured.
b.Reduced Paid up Maturity Benefit:If the Life Insured is alive on the Maturity Date, We will pay Reduced Paid Up Sum Assured on Maturity. In case the option of accrual of Survival Benefits has been chosen by You, accrued Survival Benefit, if any and if not already paid, shall be paid additionally.
c.Reduced Paid up Survival Benefit:Guaranteed Income or Guaranteed Money Back payment, as applicable, shall be equal to applicable rate of Guaranteed Income or Guaranteed Money Back (as the case may be) multiplied by Reduced Paid Up Sum Assured on Maturity.
d.Reduced Paid Up Surrender Value:The Surrender Value of reduced paid up Policy will be determined as per the applicable surrender clause.
