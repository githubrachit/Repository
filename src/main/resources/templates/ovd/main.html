As per the Govt's regulations, it's the responsibility of the customer to provide either of the following OVD's within 3 months of policy issuance. Further, in future, Govt. may ask mandate insurers to make the policy accounts "in-operative", where KYC is not completed/OVD is not received. Hence, you are requested to submit the OVD as early as possible within a maximum time frame of 3 months from the date of Policy Issuance.
OVD -
A - Passport
B - Voter's Identity Card issued by Election Commission of India
C - Driving License
D - Job card issued by NREGA duly signed by an officer of the State Government
E - Letter issued by UIDAI or National Population Register (NPR) containing details of name, address and Aadhar number.
F - Proof of possession of Aadhar number - the physical copy of e-Aadhar, masked Aadhar and Offline Aadhar XML or as may be decided by the Authority (UIDAI) from time to time.
