5 Policy Coverage/benefits payable • Benefits payable on Maturity:

➢ We will pay the applicable Fund Value on the Maturity Date.

 Clause 1.2 of Part C
• Benefits payable on Death:

➢ In case of death of the Life Insured during the Policy Term, provided the Policy is in force, We will pay the higher of the following as a Death Benefit:
  1. Sum Assured on Death (after deducting Partial Withdrawal, made during the two years' period immediately preceding the death of the Life Insured); or
  2. 105 % of Total Premium Paid; or
  3. Fund Value after adding all the charges (except for Fund Management Charge) recovered after the date of death of the Life Insured.
No Settlement Option will be provided towards the Death Benefit.

 Clause 1.1 of Part C
• Survival Benefits excluding that payable on maturity:

➢ No survival benefits are payable in the Policy.

 Clause 1.4 of Part C
• Surrender Benefits:

➢ Surrender Benefits are classified into two categories:
  1. Surrender within the Lock-in Period: In case of Surrender within the Lock-in Period, the Fund Value will be credited into the Discontinuance Policy Fund post deducting applicable Discontinuance/Surrender Charge and the applicable Fund Value shall be paid on the expiry of the said Lock-in Period. Upon Surrender, the risk cover under the Policy and applicable Riders (if any), will cease.

  2. Surrender after completion of the Lock-in Period: In case of Surrender post completion of the Lock-in Period, the applicable Fund Value prevailing on the date of receipt of a valid request for Surrender shall be paid.

6 Options available (in case of Linked Insurance Products) • Partial Withdrawal:

➢ You are entitled for the Partial Withdrawal under the Policy, subject to the following:

  1. The minimum withdrawal amount should be at least INR 5,000 and minimum age should be at least 18 years.
  2. No partial withdrawals are allowed in the first five policy years and thereafter a maximum of two partial withdrawals are allowed in a Policy Year, such that the summation of percentages of Fund Value withdrawn is not more than 50% (Fifty percent).
  3. The maximum amount of Partial Withdrawal allowed in any policy year is 50% of the Fund Value as on the date of the Partial Withdrawal(s) and subject to the Fund Value immediately after the Partial Withdrawal(s) being at least equal to one Annualised Premium or 25% of Single Premium Payment Variant, as applicable.
  4. The Sum Assured on Death shall be reduced by all Partial Withdrawals made with respect to the Fund Value from the base Premium during the 2 (Two) Policy Years immediately preceding the Life Insured's death.
 Clause 4.3 of Part D
• Top–up Provision:

➢ Top-up premiums are not accepted under the Policy.

 Clause 4.2 of Part D
• Switches:

➢ You may switch from one fund to another by written request to us, provided:

i. the minimum amount to be switched is at least Rs. 5,000;
ii. no charge shall be levied to process request for switching Units between different Funds.
iii. not more than 12 (Twelve) switches in any Policy Year.
iv. Switching will not be allowed during the period of Discontinuance, but allowed during Settlement Period.
• Premium Redirection:

You may redirect renewal Premium amongst available Funds by placing a written request, specifying the amount/ proportion of Premium to be paid into each Fund, before your Premium payment due date. There is a limit of 6 Premium Redirections request during the Policy Year.

 Clause 4.1 of Part D
• Settlement Option:

This option is available wherein maturity benefit may be received in instalments upon providing a written request to Us, specifying the proposed duration and frequency of payment. The period under the Settlement Option shall not exceed 5 (Five) years from the Maturity Date. During the period in which Settlement Option is in force, the Policy will continue and applicable Fund Management Charge, Switching charges and Mortality Charge on Death Benefit will be deducted. No Partial Withdrawals are allowed during such period.

8 Riders opted, if any
• Summary of coverage:

➢ Axis Max Life Critical Illness and Disability Secure Rider (UIN:) : Provides additional lump sum benefit depending on the diagnosis of critical illnesses of the Life Insured (major or minor critical illness, as applicable).

➢ Axis Max Life Smart Ultra Protect Rider (UIN:) : This Rider provides the following Rider Benefit variants:

  1. Rider benefit variant - Term Booster with Accelerated Terminal Illness: Provides additional lump sum benefit in the case of death or diagnosis of Terminal Illness of the Life Insured, whichever is earlier.
  2. Rider benefit variant - Accidental Death Benefit: Provides additional lump sum benefit in the case of death due to accident of the Life Insured.
  3. Rider benefit variant - Accidental Total and Permanent Disability: Provides additional lump sum benefit in the case of occurrence of total and permanent disability to the Life Insured due to accident.
  4. Rider benefit variant - Payor Benefit: Provides funding of all future outstanding base premiums and waiver of all the future premiums under all attached Riders in the case of death or diagnosis of Terminal Illness of the Policyholder, whichever is earlier.

➢ No riders are opted
