• Death Benefit

➢ If the Life Insured dies during the Policy Term, provided all due Premiums have been paid and the Policy is in force and is not a Lapsed Policy or in Reduced Paid Up Mode, on the date of Death of the Life Insured, We will pay the lump sum death benefit as follows:
  1. For Elite Variant, higher of:
    • Sum Assured on Death;
    • 105% (One Hundred Five percent) of (Total Premiums Paid plus loadings for modal premiums plus Underwriting Extra Premium (if any) plus discount (if any)) under the Policy, as on the date of death of the Life Insured; or
    • Accrued Benefit as on the date of death of the Life Insured.
    2.
  2. For Lite Variant, higher of:
    • Sum Assured on Death;
    • 105% (One Hundred Five percent) of (Total Premiums Paid plus loadings for modal premiums plus Underwriting Extra Premium (if any) plus discount (if any)) under the Policy, as on the date of death of the Life Insured; or
    • 100% (Hundred percent) of (Total Premiums Paid plus loadings for modal premiums plus Underwriting Extra Premium (if any)) plus discount (if any) as on the date of death of Life Insured plus accrued Wellness Booster (if any) plus accrued Loyalty Booster.
    3.
Clause 1.1 of Part C
• Maturity Benefit:

➢ If the Life Insured has survived until the Maturity Date, provided the Policy is in-force and has been fully paid and is not a Lapsed Policy on the Maturity Date, we will pay the following applicable Maturity benefit to You:
  1. For Elite Variant - Maturity benefit payable shall be equal to Sum Assured on Maturity. Additionally, an amount equal to the following shall be payable on maturity:
    1. Accrued Loyalty Booster (if any), plus
    2. Accumulated Saving Boosters (if any), plus
    3. Accrued Wellness Booster (if any), less
    4. Total withdrawals availed under the Health Care Benefit option, (if any).

    Note: Loyalty Booster shall be added to the maturity benefit post payment of each Premium as per the chosen Premium payment frequency.
    2.
  2. For Lite Variant - Maturity benefit payable shall be equal to:
    1. Sum Assured on Maturity, plus
    2. Accrued Loyalty Booster (if any), plus
    3. Accrued Wellness Booster, if any.

    Note: At the end of each Policy Year during the Premium Payment Term, if all due Premiums have been paid, an annual Loyalty Booster shall be added to the maturity benefit.
    3.
Clause 1.2 Of Part C
• Other benefits

➢ During the Premium Payment Term and provided the Policy is in-force and the Life Insured is alive, if the Life Insured is hospitalised, following benefits shall be payable to You depending on the type of the Hospitalisation:
  1. Fixed Daily Hospitalisation Cash Benefit: Subject to the conditions as specified below, if the Life Insured is hospitalised during the Premium Payment Term for Elite Variant and during Policy Term for Lite Variant for a minimum, continuous and complete period of 24 hours, We will pay the following Fixed Daily Hospitalisation Cash Benefit amount for each day of Hospitalisation from the first day of Hospitalisation, provided all due Premiums have been paid:

    Hospitalisation Type Benefit Payable
    Non - ICU (Intensive Care Unit) Benefit amount shall be equal to 1% of the Sum Assured for each day of Hospitalisation
    ICU (Intensive Care Unit) Benefit amount shall be equal to 2% of the Sum Assured for each day of Hospitalisation in the Intensive Care Unit (ICU)

    ➢ The Conditions for the Fixed Daily Hospitalisation Cash Benefit:
    1. The Fixed Daily Hospitalisation Cash Benefit amount per day shall be 1% percentage of the Sum Assured as on the Date of Commencement of the Risk, and shall remain constant throughout the Premium Payment Term for Elite Variant and during Policy Term for Lite Variant.
    2. Fixed Daily Hospitalisation Cash Benefit is payable with a maximum limit of 30 days per Policy Year.
    3. The specified number of days per Policy Year is inclusive of days hospitalised in non-ICU and ICU.
    4. The Fixed Daily Hospitalisation Cash Benefit for Elite variant will cease on the earlier of (i) claiming total of 300 days of Hospitalization or (2) at the end of Premium Payment Term; and for Lite variant it will cease on the earlier of (i) claiming total of 300 days of Hospitalization or (2) at the end of Policy Term.
    5. This Fixed Daily Hospitalisation Cash Benefit shall be payable subject to the following:
      1. This Benefit is payable only after a continuous and complete 24 hours of Hospitalization during the Premium Payment Term for Elite variant and during the Policy Term for Lite variant of the Life Insured for each claim.
      2. The Hospitalization must be for the Medically Necessary Treatment for an Illness/Accident and is commenced and continued on the written advice of the treating Medical Practitioner.
      3. We will not be liable to pay the daily Fixed Daily Hospitalisation Cash Benefit amount for more than the maximum number of days as specified, during the Premium Payment Term for Elite Variant and during Policy Term for Lite Variant.
      4. A Waiting Period for Fixed Daily Hospitalisation Cash Benefit has been completed prior to the Hospitalization related to Illness. No benefit shall be payable for any Hospitalisation due to Illness during the Waiting Period for Fixed Daily Hospitalisation Cash Benefit. Such a Waiting Period for Fixed Daily Hospitalisation Cash Benefit is not applicable to claims arising due to Accident provided the Accident occurs after the Date of Commencement of Risk or Policy Revival date as the case may be.
      5. More than one claim can be considered in respect of the Life Insured under the Fixed Daily Hospitalisation Cash Benefit during the Premium Payment Term for Elite Variant and during Policy Term for Lite Variant, subject to the maximum number of days specified. On exhaustion of the maximum number of days allowed, the cover under the Fixed Daily Hospitalisation Cash Benefit will terminate in relation to such Life Insured.
      6. Once claim is considered admissible as per the terms and condition under the Fixed Daily Hospitalisation Cash Benefit, You will be eligible for the fixed daily amount from the first day of Hospitalisation, provided that the Life Insured is hospitalised for a continuous period of 24 hours.
      7. Fixed Daily Hospitalisation Cash Benefit amount is a fixed per-day benefit and will be calculated on the basis of the number of continuous and completed days of Hospitalization. Fixed Daily Hospitalisation Cash Benefit will be paid as a lumpsum amount, irrespective of actual Hospitalisation expenses.
      8. Any procedure / treatment which leads to Hospitalisation of less than 24 hours is not covered.
    6. The applicable exclusions to the Fixed Daily Hospitalisation Cash Benefit are as detailed in Annexure B.
  2. Fixed Surgical Care Benefit: Subject to the conditions as specified below, if the Life Insured is hospitalised during the Premium Payment Term for Elite Variant and during the Policy Term for Lite Variant for a minimum continuous and complete period of 24 hours for undergoing Medically Necessary Treatment through surgery in India and actually undergoes that surgery while Hospitalised, We will pay the following Fixed Surgical Care Benefit amount to You for the following types of surgery, provided all due Premiums have been paid:

    Surgery Type Benefit payable
    Major Surgery Benefit amount shall be equal to the 10% of the Sum Assured
    Minor Surgery Benefit amount shall be equal to the 5% of the Sum Assured

    ➢ This Fixed Surgical Care Benefit shall be payable subject to the following:
    1. You can claim multiple times under this Policy during the Premium Payment Term irrespective of any previous claims paid by Us. However, in no circumstance shall We pay more than the following limits:
      • 20% of Sum Assured in any one Policy Year; and
      • 100% of Sum Assured during the Premium Payment Term for Elite Variant and during Policy Term for Lite Variant.
    2. The Fixed Surgical Care Benefit for Elite Variant will cease to be available on the earlier of (i) claiming 100% of the Sum Assured; or (ii) at the end of Premium Payment Term and for Lite Variant it will cease on the earlier of (i) claiming 100% of chosen Sum Assured or (ii) at the end of Policy Term.
    3. Liability to make any payment under the Surgical Care Benefit shall commence only after a continuous and completed 24 hours of Hospitalization of the Life Insured for each claim.
    4. The surgery must be for Medically Necessary Treatment of any Illness or Injury due to an Accident and is commenced and continued on the written advice of the treating Medical Practitioner.
    5. We will not be liable to pay the daily Fixed Surgical Care Benefit amount for more than the maximum limit as specified, during the Premium Payment Term for Elite Variant and during Policy Term for Lite Variant.
    6. A Waiting Period for Fixed Surgical Care Benefit has been completed prior to the Hospitalization for surgery related to Illness. No benefit shall be payable for any Fixed Surgical Care Benefit claim due to Illness during the Waiting Period for Fixed Surgical Care Benefit. The Waiting Period for Fixed Surgical Care Benefit will not be applicable to claims arising due to Accident provided the Accident occurs after the Date of Commencement of Risk of the Policy or Policy Revival date, as the case may be.
    7. More than one claim can be considered in respect of the Life Insured under this Fixed Surgical Care Benefit during the Premium Payment Term for Elite Variant and during Policy Term for Lite Variant, subject to the maximum limit specified. On exhaustion of the maximum limit allowed, the cover under the Fixed Surgical Care Benefit will terminate in relation to such Life Insured.
    8. The Fixed Surgical Care Benefit amount shall be paid based on the surgery resulting in highest claim amount in case of more than one surgery is performed on the Life Insured during the same Hospitalisation.
    9. The Fixed Surgical Care Benefit is a fixed lumpsum benefit, irrespective of actual surgery expenses.
    10. Any procedure / treatment which leads to Hospitalisation of less than 24 hours is not covered.
    11. The applicable exclusions to the Fixed Surgical Care Benefit are as detailed in Annexure C.

14 Policy Loan, if applicable ➢ Once this Policy has acquired the Surrender Value, then, You will be eligible for grant of loans from Us wherein:

Once the Policy acquires a Surrender Value, you are eligible for a loan.
  1. Loans are available only during the Policy Term.
  2. The minimum loan amount is INR 10,000, with a maximum of 75% of the Surrender Value.
  3. Upon loan approval, the Policy will be assigned to us until the loan, including interest and fees, is repaid. Upon repayment, the Policy will be reassigned to you.
  4. All benefits and withdrawals under the Health Care Benefit Option will first be adjusted to any outstanding loan and accrued interest. Payouts from the Fixed Daily Hospitalization Cash Benefit and Fixed Surgical Care Benefit will not be adjusted against the loan.
  5. Loan interest will be compounded annually at the rates set by us at the time of the loan.
  6. The loan interest rate is the applicable RBI Bank Rate plus 3.0 % (currently 9.75% p.a.), based on the RBI Bank Rate of 6.75% as of March 31, 2024. This rate is reviewed annually and is effective from July 1 to June 30. Any changes will require IRDAI approval.
  7. Inforce or fully paid-up policies will not be foreclosed for non-payment of the loan, even if the outstanding balance exceeds the Surrender Value.
  8. For Reduced Paid-Up Mode Policies, if the outstanding loan and interest exceed the Surrender Value, the Policy will terminate immediately. If the outstanding loan exceeds 95% of the Surrender Value or if the remaining Policy Term is 6 months or less, we will notify you for repayment.
